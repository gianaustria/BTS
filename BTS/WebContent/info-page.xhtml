Our Mission

Our website aims to promote the national heritage of our country, the beautiful seas of the Philippines. We aim to help small-time souvenir shops gain profits. We also aim to promote marine life rehabilitation, since our marine life would tend to decline due to climate change. We human should help preserve and save our marine life for they are one of our main basic resources.

Our Vision

To spread awareness about the situation our marine life is facing throughout the country. The people of our country should be able to know the good and bad things happening to our seas.
