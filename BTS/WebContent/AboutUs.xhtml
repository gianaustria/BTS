About Us

Welcome explorer! We are the explorers under the sea, yes we hunt, we hunt different species of fish around the Philippines! If you want to see and learn more about the fishes and the life under the sea, you are on the right place.

You are free to do anything, we accept donations and this would be given to our partner NGOs. We also help our local markets in the different beaches in the Philippines by selling souvenirs. Now let us meet the team and get to know them better!!
